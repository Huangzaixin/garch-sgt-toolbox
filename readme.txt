Purpose: This Matlab toolbox helps readers to reproduce the Chinese paper "基于SGT分布的ES估计、后验分析及在沪深股市中应用"，数理统计与管理，2020，39(2). The SGT distribution refers to the skewed generalized t distribution introduced by Theodossiou (1998). See the paper "Financial data and the skewed generalized t distribution", Management science, 1998, 44(12-part-1): 1650-1661.
